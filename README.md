“Wun geoi” (玩具) means toy in Cantonese. When I asked my 2-year-old son what matters most to him right now, he simply repeated: “wun geoi, wun geoi.” Inspired by that simplicity and focus, wunkui — with a similar pronunciation — is the name of this core time-series forecasting toolbox. Like a toy, it is designed to be a flexible, foundational building block for powering a variety of downstream tasks.
